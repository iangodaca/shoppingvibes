About Us

Welcome to our website! This site is dedicated to providing an overview of the best shopping malls in Bacolod City. We aim to help shoppers and tourists discover great places to shop, dine, and enjoy their time in the province.

Bacolod has a rich shopping culture with modern malls, local markets, and unique stores that offer everything from branded fashion to local delicacies. Whether you're a local or a visitor, our guide will help you explore the best shopping destinations.
