888 Chinatown Square Bacolod

888 Chinatown is a famous shopping mall in Bacolod City. It is the local version of the renowned Divisoria market in Manila, where you can find practically everything you want ... clothes apparel, fashion accessories, electronic gadgets, eateries, DVDs, etc. at a reasonable price. You can also haggle for a lower price! It has dozens and dozens of alleys that constitute a real maze for shoppers. I suggest that you go there with somebody familiar with the place so as to have a guide through its intricate hallways. Also keep an eye on your personal belongings for pick-pocketing is quite common in the area. 888 is one place you should visit when you go to Bacolod City. It gives a feel of the local shopping experience.
