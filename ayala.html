Ayala Malls Capitol Central

Ayala Malls Capitol Central strengthens the Negrense tradition by showcasing the best home-grown concepts. Enhancing the shopping experience through a mix of local and global offerings, alongside it’s key features of the Fiesta Negrense, the Central Hall, the Food Choices, the Central Bazaar, the Chapel and the Corte, it becomes the perfect location to celebrate small winnings, major milestones, and intimate gatherings. The mall was walking distance from where we were billeted so it was convenient for us to drop by for emergencies or snacks. It’s a fair sized mall with a wide variety of restaurants to choose from. I liked the area where they were selling the native delicacies . But when you look at it, there really wasn’t anything extra special about the mall. Other than it’s proximity to our hotel making it convenient, there’s really nothing much more for me to say .
